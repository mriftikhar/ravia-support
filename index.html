Getting Started

Create an account, list an item with photos and details, and you’re ready to sell. Buyers can add items to cart or send an offer.

Payments

All payments are processed securely with Stripe. Sellers receive payouts through Stripe Connect after a successful sale.

Shipping

Real‑time shipping rates and estimates are shown at checkout. Some sellers may offer free shipping or thresholds.

Offers

Buyers can submit offers when enabled by the seller. Accepted offers proceed to secure checkout through the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do payouts work for sellers?

Payouts are handled by Stripe Connect to the bank account configured in Stripe. Timing depends on your Stripe account settings.

How is my payment information secured?

Ravia never stores full card details. Stripe handles payment tokenization and processing over secure connections.

How are shipping costs calculated?

At checkout, we fetch real‑time carrier rates and apply any seller free‑shipping rules or thresholds when eligible.
